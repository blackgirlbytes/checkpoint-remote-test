Feed newborns every 2-3 hours, whether breast or bottle, watching for hunger cues like rooting and hand-sucking.
Always place babies on their back to sleep on a firm, flat surface with no loose bedding or soft toys.
Support the baby's head and neck at all times since their muscles are not yet strong enough to hold it up.
Keep the umbilical cord stump clean and dry, letting it fall off naturally within one to three weeks.
